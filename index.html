PRIVACY POLICY · DRAFT

DataDost Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026 · Designed in alignment with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023

⚠️ Beta notice: DataDost is currently in early beta testing. This policy describes our intended practices and design principles. Formal entity registration and full DPDP Act compliance mechanisms are in progress and will be completed before public launch.
Our Four Core Commitments
✓ We never store your original documents. Bank statements and PDFs are read and deleted immediately after analysis.
✓ We never sell your data to any third party — ever. No advertisers, no banks, no government without your explicit consent.
✓ We never share your financial information without your knowledge and consent.
✓ You can delete all your data instantly, anytime, with one click.
🤝 Sharing your data is entirely your choice
Uploading a document or asking DataDost a question is always voluntary, at your sole discretion, and reversible. By choosing to upload a document, you are giving DataDost explicit permission to read and parse that specific document for the sole purpose of answering your question. We do not access, request, or parse any data you have not actively chosen to share with us in that moment. You may stop sharing, delete what you've shared, or close your account at any time, with no obligation to continue.
1. What data we collect
We collect only what is necessary to provide the DataDost service: your name and email address (for your account), extracted financial insights from documents you upload (spending categories, totals, balances — never the raw document), and your questions and DataDost's responses within your session. We do not collect Aadhaar numbers, PAN numbers, bank account numbers, card numbers, or any government identification.
2. How we process your documents
When you upload a document, it is temporarily processed by our AI system (Anthropic Claude API) to extract financial insights. The raw document file is permanently deleted from our servers within seconds of processing. Only the extracted insights (e.g., "food delivery spend: ₹4,820", "closing balance: ₹3,820") are stored in your account — never the original PDF or image.
3. Where your data is stored
All user account data and financial insights are stored on servers located in India (Mumbai region, AWS ap-south-1) in compliance with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023. Your data does not leave Indian territory. All data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption and in transit using TLS 1.3.
4. Your rights under DPDP Act 2023
You have the right to: access all data DataDost holds about you, correct any inaccurate data, delete your data permanently (in full or by individual item), withdraw consent at any time, and nominate someone to exercise these rights on your behalf. On the History screen, use the download icon to export your data, the trash icon to delete specific items, or "Clear everything at once" to remove all your data immediately — each comes with a clear warning before anything happens. (Note: this clears your data, not your login itself — to fully close your account as well, email us and we'll do that by hand for now.) For anything else, email us at privacy@datadost.in.
5. Third-party services
DataDost uses the following third-party services to operate: Anthropic (AI processing — your document content is transmitted to Anthropic's API for analysis, subject to Anthropic's privacy policy), Supabase (database and authentication — data stored in India region), and Vercel (website hosting — static files only, no personal data). Razorpay (payment processing) is planned for a future paid tier and is not yet active — we never see your full card number when it is. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any advertising tracking tools.
6. If something ever goes wrong — breach notification
In the unlikely event of a personal data breach, we will notify every affected user directly (email, not just a website notice) and report it to India's Data Protection Board, in line with DPDP Act requirements — regardless of how small the breach is, with no minimum threshold for when this applies. We treat this as a real operational commitment, not boilerplate: our incident response plan is built around the same 6-hour reporting window CERT-In requires for serious cybersecurity incidents.
7. Grievance Officer & how we handle concerns
As required under the DPDP Act 2023, our designated Grievance Officer can be contacted at: privacy@datadost.in. Every complaint gets a reference number and an automatic acknowledgment, and we commit to resolving it within 30 days — well inside the 90-day maximum the DPDP Rules allow. If you're ever unsatisfied with our response, you have the right to escalate directly to India's Data Protection Board.

FAQ

How we handle your data

The plain-language version. For the full legal detail, see the Privacy Policy above.

Do you save my bank statement or salary slip?
No. The moment DataDost finishes reading it — usually a few seconds — the file itself is deleted from our servers for good. We never keep the original PDF or photo.
Then what exactly do you save?
A short summary of what was in it — your spending by category, your balance, things like that — plus your chat history with DataDost. Never your raw account number, card number, Aadhaar, or PAN. We keep this so DataDost can pick up where you left off next time, instead of starting from zero.
Do I have to re-upload every time I come back?
No — that's exactly what the summary above is for. Reopen an old conversation and DataDost already remembers your numbers and what you discussed. You'd only upload again to ask about a different document.
Can you see my actual bank account?
No, never. DataDost never asks for your net banking login or any account access. You choose what to upload, and that's the only thing we ever see.
Can I delete everything?
Yes, yourself, anytime — no need to email anyone first. On your History screen, the download and trash icons let you pick specific conversations or documents, or use "Clear everything at once" to wipe all of it — either way, you'll always see a clear warning before anything is actually deleted.
Do you sell my data or show me ads?
No. Not to advertisers, not to banks, not to anyone, ever. We don't use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any ad-tracking tool either. Our plan is paid subscriptions, not your data.
Is my data safe?
It's stored only in India (Mumbai), encrypted both at rest and in transit. If anything ever did go wrong, you'd hear from us directly — not just a notice buried on a website.
Who do I talk to if I'm not happy with an answer?
Email privacy@datadost.in. Every message gets a reference number and a response within 30 days — faster than the law requires.
